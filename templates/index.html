About NeuroKey

NeuroKey is a research-backed screening tool developed at VIT Pune that leverages keystroke dynamics — a non-invasive biometric — to detect subtle motor control changes associated with early Parkinson's disease.

Parkinson's disease affects over 10 million people worldwide. Motor symptoms often appear years before clinical diagnosis. By analyzing the way you type, we can identify patterns that correlate with early neurological changes.

Non-invasive screening

No blood tests, no equipment — just a keyboard and 90 seconds.

PSO-selected features

Particle Swarm Optimization identifies the 3 most predictive keystroke features.

Ensemble ML model

Voting + Stacking classifier combining Random Forest, SVM, and Gradient Boosting.

Model Pipeline

1

Data Collection

Keystroke dynamics captured from 200+ subjects in archived sessions.

2

Feature Engineering

Extraction of mean/std hold time, mean/std flight time, hold-to-flight ratio.

3

PSO Feature Selection

BinaryPSO selects optimal feature subset to maximize classification accuracy.

4

Ensemble Classifier

VotingClassifier (soft) with 5-fold cross validation. Scaler and model saved as .pkl.

5

Report Generation

Comparison analysis, confidence score, and PDF export for clinical use.
